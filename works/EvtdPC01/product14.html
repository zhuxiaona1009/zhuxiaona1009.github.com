BMS Product-BEBM Series BMS for sedan cars and mid-size cars
1.Brief Introduction
    This BMS product is for li-ion batteries in EV buses, hybrid buses, and large equipment of high voltage. Its function includes testing the voltage, temperature, and calculation of SOC of the individual cells in one battery pack, read charge/discharge current information from external current sensors. The main body of the product connects with other cells in the pack by wirings and the external power by power supply wirings. In addition, it has CAN Communication, Current Test Input, and Temperature Test Input ports. By sending signals to VMS or other units through CAN, software can be installed in the ROM inside the CPU. In addition to the standard software configuration, storage setting can be modified according to customers’ needs.
2.Product features:
・Safety: Constant monitoring and warning in advance to prevent accidents.
・Portability: Adopt one-stop control, reduce the weight of the system substantially.
・Stability: Stable running system, low runtime error rate, and low signal occupation rate.
・Reliability: Read all kinds of data correctly and send the data to other units precisely.
・Economy: Match with various kinds of batteries, extend battery life and lower use cost.
