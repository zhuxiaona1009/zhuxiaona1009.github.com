Company Introduction
     EVTD, Inc. is a high-tech company dedicated to research, production, sales, and service of Battery Management System (BMS) and consulting service related to Li-ion battery application technology. We have a professional service team proficient in battery, electrical machinery, and automobiles and has rich knowledge and experience in the area of Li-ion battery application and BMS. Today we have obtained 16 patents related to Li-ion battery Management System development and application technology.
     Many of our management and technology team members are graduates from worldly-renowned universities including Peking University, University of Pennsylvania, London School of Economics and Political Science, The University of Tokyo, Waseda University, and Kyoto University. To execute advanced management concepts and exploit the advantage of global human resource, we have established research and experiment centers in Shanghai and Tokyo and amicable collaboration relationship with famous institutions and universities to apply the latest research outcome to products.
     EVTD, Inc. also collaborates with famous production companies of vehicle parts around the world at the same time consolidating our research team. By taking advantage of each company’s strengths, we have developed a fully automatic production system capable of producing millions of BMS and clips special for balancing systems per year. This system has passed the certifications of ISO9001 and ISO14001.
